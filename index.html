Our Global Legacy

As a global leader in diamond trade, DIATAJ maintains strategic operations across multiple international markets, demonstrating our commitment to sustainable global commerce. Through the implementation of industry-leading sustainable practices in each of our locations, we ensure that our business growth directly enhances the prosperity of our partner companies and stakeholders. Our unwavering dedication to sustainability continues to drive innovation and responsible leadership in the premium diamond industry." This version maintains the core message while using more sophisticated language and emphasizing the company's industry leadership and professional standing in the diamond trade.

  • Pioneering International Markets: Since 1996, DIATAJ has established itself as a distinguished player in the international rough diamond market. Our journey began in Johannesburg, South Africa, where we launched our first rough diamond operations, marking the beginning of our global expansion.
  • Strategic Growth & Market Development: Building on our initial success, we strategically expanded our operations to include both tender-based acquisitions and local market partnerships. This diversified approach has enabled us to develop a comprehensive understanding of various market dynamics and establish strong relationships with key stakeholders across the diamond industry..
  • Global Network of Excellence: Today, DIATAJ maintains a prestigious presence across three continents, with strategic operations in:
    • Antwerp: The historic center of global diamond trade
    • South Africa: Our founding location
    • Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC): Access to premium rough diamonds
    • Dubai: Our UAE headquarters in the prestigious Almas Tower
    • Moscow: Strategic presence in the Russian market
    • Mumbai & Surat, India: Leveraging these cities' renowned diamond processing expertise
Nilesh Shah, the esteemed founder and owner of DIA TAJ, brings over 30 years of distinguished membership with the Diamond Bourse of India.
